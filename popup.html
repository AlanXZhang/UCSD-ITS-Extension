Shortcuts

  • Bold: ⌘+B or Ctrl+B
  • Italic: ⌘+I or Ctrl+I
  • Hyperlink: ⌘+K or Ctrl+Q
  • Image: ⌘+Shift+I or Ctrl+Shift+I
  • Code: ⌘+Shift+C or Ctrl+Shift+C
  • Blockquote: ⌘+Shift+B or Ctrl+Shift+B
  • Ordered List: ⌘+O or Ctrl+O
  • Unordered List: ⌘+U or Ctrl+U
  • List Item: ⌘+L or Ctrl+L
  • Paragraph: ⌘+P or Ctrl+P
  • Propose Solution Confirmation: ⌘+Shift+X or Ctrl+Shift+X to toggle.
  • Copy Case System ID: ⌘+Shift+C or Ctrl+Shift+C
